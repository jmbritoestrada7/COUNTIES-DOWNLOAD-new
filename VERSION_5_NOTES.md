# County Map Generator — Version 5

## New features

- Permanent Cloudflare R2 project storage.
- Automatic migration of existing projects without changing their permanent links.
- English-only interface.
- Named polygons, rectangles, and circles.
- Drawn Areas panel with focus, hide/show, rename, and delete controls.
- Autosaved STR filters and layer visibility.
- Excel (`.xlsx`, `.xlsm`) and CSV (`.csv`) uploads.
- Only `County` and `State` are required in uploaded files.
- Counties without STR display in neutral gray as `NO DATA`.
- Any inactive county can be clicked and activated manually.
- Manually activated counties persist in R2 and support notes, priority, assignee, and next review.
- Uploading STR later automatically changes a neutral county to its STR range color.
